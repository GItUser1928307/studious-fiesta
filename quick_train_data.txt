<q> What is a derivative?
<a> A derivative measures how a function changes. It is the slope of the function at a specific point.
<q> What is an integral?
<a> An integral is the reverse of differentiation. It calculates the area under a curve.
<q> What is infinity?
<a> Infinity is a concept of something without bound or end. It is not a specific number but a limit.
<q> What is zero factorial?
<a> Zero factorial equals 1 by definition. 0! = 1.
<q> What is 5 factorial?
<a> 5 factorial equals 120. 5! = 5 times 4 times 3 times 2 times 1 = 120.
<q> What is a combination?
<a> A combination is a selection of items where order does not matter. It counts subsets of a larger set.
<q> What is a permutation?
<a> A permutation is an arrangement of items where order matters. It counts ordered arrangements.
